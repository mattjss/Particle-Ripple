USE THIS PROJECT TO RUN THE APP
================================

1. Open Finder and go to: Particle Ripple → RippleApp
2. Double-click: RippleApp.xcodeproj
3. In Xcode, pick a simulator (e.g. iPhone 16) at the top
4. Press ⌘R to build and run

When the app opens: drag or tap on the black screen to create ripples in the dot grid.
